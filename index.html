About Me

I am a Biomedical Engineering graduate from the University of South Florida, specializing in the application of software to solve complex healthcare challenges. My experience ranges from developing MATLAB and Python scripts for neuronal network analysis at the Byrd's Alzheimer Institute to engineering a wearable macrofluidic device for cancer immunotherapy at Moffitt Cancer Center.

I am driven to leverage technologies like C++, microcontrollers, and SOLIDWORKS to build tangible solutions, and I thrive in collaborative environments where I can apply my unique blend of wet lab experience and coding skills. I'm eager to join an innovative team and contribute to impactful projects.
